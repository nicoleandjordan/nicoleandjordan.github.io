FAQs

When can I arrive?
The ceremony is scheduled to start at 12pm, and guests are to be seated by 11:30am. All are welcome to the ceremony. If you are joining us for our evening celebration, please arrive from 7pm onwards.

What should I wear?
Please dress to impress in formal attire for the ceremony and daytime reception. You're welcome to bring some more comfortable shoes for the evening celebration to make it easier to move on the dance floor. We certainly will be!

Can I take photos and videos?
You are more than welcome to take as many photos and videos during the day as you would like, but we kindly ask that you refrain from doing so during the ceremony. We will have professional photographers and videographers to do this for you so that you are able to enjoy the ceremony. We would love for you to share your photos and videos with us, so we'll provide QR codes on and after the day for you to upload them.

Are the venues accessible?
The church has a side entrance with a ramp and an accessible toilet at the back. At the hotel, there are no stairs or steps between the entrance and the Great Hall and accessible toilets and rooms are available.
